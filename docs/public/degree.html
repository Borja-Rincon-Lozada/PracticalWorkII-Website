Degree overview

Computer Engineering + Business Analytics

This degree had been eliminated from our collage, but this doesn't mean it is a waste of time and money. It has a lot of job opportunities because of the approach made by the combination of Busyness Analytics and Computer Engineering. Also we have several references of elder students about how this degree is not as bad as it seems after the elimination of the degree.

Purpose

The main purpose of this degree is to integrate two of the degrees with a proffesional projection expected to go to the moon. This by incorporating learning through challenges of different sciences (Law, Business, Economics and Government). For us to have the capacity to face problematics related to new digital era.

Structure

You can understand the structure in two ways:

By degree: Business & Data Analytics is the “base”, and Computer Engineering adds the technical layer on top.

By years: The double degree is distributed across multiple years. Most years include subjects from both areas, and the last stage focuses more on the engineering side. It is set to be completed in 5 years and a half. That half year is because of the collage, a different approach to us in our first part of the first year of the degree, leading us to make an extra half year to catch up.
